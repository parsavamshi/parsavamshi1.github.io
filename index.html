What is Bingo?

We are BINGO which is the first pan entertainment platform for decentralized blockchain in the world. It is
based on the technology of blockchain, and through the social features of the blockchain such as heavy
user agglutinant, frequent communications and game players with communities and so on, we build up
the new generation pan entertainment platform: BINGO. It contains: Customer Management System, BINGO
Wallet, Traffic Exchange System, Decentralized Data Analysis System and BINGO Foundation.

WHAT WILL BINGO DO?

The World's First Blockchain Pan Entertainment Platform

Asynchronous Fairness
Verifying Module

Develop the Asynchronous Fairness Verifying Module for the 777.BINGO platform, which can perfectly solve issues such as black case work and competition in unfair environments that users encounter in traditional games. The decentralized verifying module can greatly promote the users’ trust in the game and their willingness to play.

User Management
System

It consists of User Registration Module, BINGO Wallet, Assets Transaction Module (include but not limited to Game Leveling, Playmates, In-game Item Purchase, Tipping and Rewarding System) and Community Maintenance Module.

bingo wallet

Develop the own digital wallet for the platform 777.BINGO, which can solve the payment problem in the games of blockchain at present time once and for all. It covers all the main trend purchase channels used digital currency on market. In this case it will fix the situation in one-step which lacks of integrated digital wallet on the current market.

Data Analysis
System

It consists of User Registration Module, BINGO Wallet, Assets Transaction Module (include but not limited to Game Leveling, Playmates, In-game Item Purchase, Tipping and Rewarding System) and Community Maintenance Module.

Integrated Smart
Contract Translator

The Integrated Smart Contract Translator, which can realize one set of code to adopt many Smart Contract Platforms in main trend. No more research or switch in for developers which for sure will shorten the learning cost and time sharply.

Portfolio

SPACE TANKS ONLINE

It is a MOBA game which developed by Mob Arts, the technical advisor of the BINGO Pan Entertainment Platform. Early in 2011, Mob Arts has finished the prospective layout and development of mobile MOBA game which was far more advanced in the game industry. SPACE TANKS ONLINE has become so popular and aroused so much focuses after its releasing. In September 2011, NEXON, world’s top game publisher got the exclusive license from Mob Arts to publish the wonderful game to Korea and to main countries of the western world. In Korea, SPACE TANKS ONLINE topped the Korean App Store within the first week of its official launching!

House of Heroes

It is an epoch-making card RPG game which developed by Mob Arts, the technical advisor of BINGO Pan Entertainment Platform. In September 2013, as the first third party developed mobile game which was launched on full platforms of Tencent [WeChat, Mobile QQ, Myapp (Listed in no particular order)] in China, it quickly rushed to TOP 1 Grossing of App Store in China after 3 days of launching; Within the first one week of Tencent’s launching, the total download is over 10 million; the grossing revenue of the first launching month is over 19 million dollars. In total , House of Heroes gained 100 million dollars, over 50 million users!

Wrath of War

In July 2017, Mob Arts were determined to begin its Globalization Strategy in game publishing fields for the first time. The first project is the U3D strategy game ‘Wrath of War’ which was independently developed by Mob Arts team over 2 years. As the game itself was developed with a high-quality, with great art and also with a heavy brain-burning gameplay, it perfectly met the requirement of the strategy game market. After launched, it caused such a stir of all strategy players and got a public praise, the downloads kept rising and rising! Finally it also won Google Play Feature for many times!

Dragon Knight 3D

It is a 3D RPG Shooting game, which was also independently developed by Mob Arts. The main gameplay is magical flying dragon air combats, and it also perfectly fused the RPG elements into the game. In March 2015, it was launched on all social platforms of Tencent.As a partner of Tencent --- World’s Internet Giant, Mob Arts, with its 2 main works got launched by Tencent, are so honored to be as the participant and witness of the growing history of Tencent social networking Empire!!
